Printing (Crafting)

In this game, we use a lost ancient advanced technology to craft items. This technology is a 3D printer machine. It uses runes as blueprints to tell the 3D printer what are the ingredients to print an object.

Step 1: Open the printer.

Step 2: Drag the blueprint into the printer.

Step 3: Drag the ingredients into the printer.

Step 4: Drag the printed item back into the inventory.
